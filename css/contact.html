TAZU Academy Secondary School, Doma, Nasarawa State,
is a government approved and co-educational (Boys and Girls)
secondary school with full boarding and day facilities.
The school which is Government approved was established in 2013.
TAZU Academy Secondary School is located in a serene neighbourhood,
off Brumbrum Road, Doma. Our environment is free from
the distractive urban influences and particularly suited
for focused academic instruction and learning.
